Before you read

According to various studies, about 0,2-0,5% of the Earth’s surface is covered by roads. This means that we're already using a massive amount of real estate to roads.

The function of these is to allow vehicles to drive around, but other than that the roads don't do anything.

Some of the benefits from solar roadways

As noted in prevuis section the road we already have only have one function allowing us to drive around, but solar roads will add more functionality to the roads:

  1. The roads are always clear of snow.
  2. The roads are generating power from the sun and thus leaving no carbon footprint.
  3. The roads can help navigation in bad weather and they can warn drivers about people or animals walking onto the road in great time.
  4. The roads can make it easy to connect to the power grid and the internet.
  5. When the solar roadways are being implenmented you as a citizen will get lower electric bills.
